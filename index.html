Features

A tutor that knows
exactly where you are

No one-size-fits-all lessons. No cloud processing your pronunciation. Just an AI that adapts to you, privately.

🧠

On-device AI tutor

Apple's MLX framework runs the language model on your A17 Pro chip or newer. Your voice never leaves the device — not even for transcription.

📚

Adaptive proficiency memory

Lingu remembers your vocabulary gaps, recurring grammar mistakes, and learning rhythm — and adjusts every session around them.

🌍

Offline travel mode

Download your language pack (~1–2 GB) and use Lingu anywhere — no roaming, no Wi-Fi, no data charges abroad.

🔊

Direct feedback, no sugar-coating

The tutor corrects your grammar and pronunciation with precision. You learn faster when feedback is honest, not encouraging.
